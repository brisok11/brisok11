AI engineering, end to end.

I'm a Senior AI Engineer and Model Evaluation Specialist with 8+ years of experience building backend systems, APIs, automation workflows, and AI-powered solutions. My work sits at the intersection of research and production: I train and fine-tune models, build the computer vision and NLP pipelines around them, and design the evaluation frameworks that prove they actually work.

I care about systems that are reliable under real-world conditions — not just accurate on a benchmark. If AI is new to you, no worries — I can explain everything clearly and guide you through the process, from problem framing to a shipped, evaluated model.
